About us

What is Lexarithmos

Lexarithmos is a private institute offering a plethora of subjects for children ranging from grade 4 in primary school up to A-levels. Our aim is to prepare students in the best way possible and aid them in achieving their goals.

Our name comes from the concept of "Lexarithmos", where each letter has an innate value, with each word's Lexarithmos being the total value of all its letters. To calculate this sum, the word must be written in capital letters and using the table of values for each capital letter, one can find the Lexarithmos of a word. As far as we know, this concept is exclusive to the Greek language.

The Lexarithmos of the word "Lexarithmos is 1406

"Pan metron ariston"
